Premier Location

Belleair is acclaimed for its desirable location and close proximity to downtown Clearwater, world famous  sugar-sand beaches, entertainment, shopping, sports venues, renowned medical facilities and everything Tampa Bay has to offer.

Location Perfection

In addition to many of its on-site amenities and conveniences, the hidden hideaway of Belleview Place is just moments away from essential and exciting destinations throughout the Tampa Bay area, including:

  • Downtown Clearwater: 1.5 miles
  • Casual and fine dining: minutes away, including Belleair Country Club across the street
  • Award-winning Clearwater Beach: 10 minutes
  • Pinellas Trail for walking and biking: less than 1 mile
  • General and specialty grocery stores: less than 5 miles
  • Neighborhood shopping plazas: 5 to 15 minutes away
  • Tampa International Airport: 30 minutes
  • St. Pete-Clearwater International Airport: 20 minutes
  • Morton Plant Hospital: a few short blocks away
  • Entertainment at Ruth Eckerd Hall: 30 minutes
  • Numerous pro sports venues (Tampa Bay Buccaneers, Rays, Lightning and Rowdies): throughout the bay area
  • Capitol Theater: 10 minutes
