The New Belleair Address for Luxury Resort-style Living

Set high on a bluff with magnificent views of Clearwater Harbor, a new gated community of just 132 luxury condominiums and two-story carriage homes (where flood insurance is not required!). Just a few short steps from the Belleair Country Club and minutes from world-famous Clearwater Beach, Belleview Place re-introduces the elegance of the Belleview Biltmore Hotel that once graced this site.

Belleview Inn

Relocated, renovated and re-imagined for today’s visitors, the on-site Belleview Inn includes the lobby and 35 guest rooms of the original Belleview Biltmore Hotel, plus pool, fitness center, Tiffany meeting room, and Maisie’s Marketplace. Scheduled to open in 2018.
