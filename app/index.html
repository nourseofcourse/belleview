A new lifestyle of historic proportions

A new gated community of just 132 luxury condos and carriage homes, Belleview Place re-introduces the gracious living of the iconic Belleview Biltmore Hotel that once claimed this site. Set 35 feet above Clearwater Harbor and a few short steps from the Belleair Golf and Country Club. A seamless blend of historic refinement and contemporary Florida living, from a builder with a track record of excellence.
