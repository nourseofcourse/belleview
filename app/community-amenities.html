Convenience – and the Ultimate Amenity

Time, we believe, is the perfect amenity. Whether it’s time gained by living in a maintenance-free home, by shorter distances to everyday destinations like golfing or dining, or by simply reducing the frenetic pace of life, the accrued savings reward you with the luxury of time to spend with family, friends, to travel, enjoy hobbies or to savor moments of quiet reflection.

Pool

Just off the back porch of the on-site Belleview Inn [link to Inn page] and embraced by vine-draped pergolas and sundeck, the community’s 20-foot by 60-foot resort-style swimming pool is just the place to stretch, unwind and enjoy. A heated spa pool and lively water feature add soothing sound and continuous movement.

Fitness Center

A uplifting & thoughtful fitness room with essential cardio- and strength-training equipment is situated on the first floor of the Inn and overlooks the pool and beautifully landscaped Grand Lawn. The adjacent deep-set back porch is just the spot to rest and rehydrate.

Social Spaces

Inviting social settings in the Inn offer venues for meetings, parties and celebrations. Other services also are available to residents, including a catering kitchen, an informal snack shop and a History Room with displays and information about the Belleview Biltmore Hotel and its place in history. The main lobbies in the Mid-rise buildings include beautiful gathering spots as well.

Grand Lawn

Continuing the original hotel’s reputation as the Tampa Bay area’s go-to scene for weddings, corporate events and community celebrations, the 150-foot-by-60-foot landscaped Grand Lawn provides the picture-perfect outdoor setting. Located between the pool and porch, it’s easy to imagine memorable times under sun or stars.

Walking Paths

Sidewalks throughout the community offer more than a one-mile circuit of tranquil rambling within the gated community. The walkways then connect to public sidewalks that circle the golf course and lead to the 44-mile long Fred Marquis Pinellas Trail (less than a mile away).
