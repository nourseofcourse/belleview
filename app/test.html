A new lifestyle of historic proportions

A new gated community of just 132 condominium and carriage homes, Belleview Place re-introduces the gracious living of the iconic Belleview Biltmore Hotel that once claimed this site, 35 feet above Clearwater Harbor and a few short steps from the prestigious Belleair Country Club. A seamless blend of historic refinement and contemporary Florida living, from a builder with a track record of excellence.

The setting

With less than two square miles of buildable land, Belleair, Florida, maintains a character of prestige that is now home to Belleview Place. So private, and yet minutes to everything.

Life with style

Taking our cue from the historic hotel that once settled on this site, we’ve opened the door to carefree living, with ample time for family friends and a wide array of entertaining amenities.

Personal space

Homes with open floor plans, spacious master suites, light-filled windows and private terraces, plus generous choices for fine finishes — so your home is unique to your style and personality.

Reliving History in 21st-Century Style

“No other community in Florida can boast such a fascinating history — or a location on a 35-foot bluff overlooking calm waters and a beautifully landscaped golf course. We’re developing Belleview Place with great respect to both history and location, as well as to the needs of today’s homebuyers.”

J. Michael Cheezem CEO, JMC Communities
