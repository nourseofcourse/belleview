Premier location

Within the gem of Belleair lies the luxury home community of Belleview Place, accessible via a small bridge and sentry gate. A private road loops around the community and reveals the undulating landscape of two of Florida’s most beautiful championship golf courses overlooking the calm waters of Clearwater Harbor. Its location on a 35-foot bluff offers more than spectacular views; it offers significant savings plus peace of mind, as no flood insurance is required here.

Belleair is acclaimed for its desirable location and close proximity to downtown Clearwater, world famous sugar-sand beaches, entertainment, shopping, sports venues, renowned medical facilities and everything Tampa Bay has to offer.

  • 1.5 miles to downtown Clearwater
  • Minutes to casual and fine dining (including Belleair Country Club just steps away)
  • 10 minutes to the white sugar sands of award-winning Clearwater Beach
  • Less than a mile to walking and biking paths along the Pinellas Trail
  • Less than five miles to a half dozen general and specialty grocery stores
  • Numerous neighborhood shopping plazas
  • 30 minutes to Tampa International Airport
  • 20 minutes to St. Pete-Clearwater International Airport
  • A few short blocks to nationally acclaimed Morton Plant Hospital
  • 20 minutes to exceptional performing arts entertainment at Ruth Eckerd Hall
  • Numerous pro sports venues, including the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, Rays, Lightning and Rowdies.
